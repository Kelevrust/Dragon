Project Grimm: Design Bible

1. Visual Identity

Theme: Neon Noir / Urban Folklore.
Setting: "The Sprawl" - A modern metropolis inhabited by myths hiding in plain sight.
Style: High contrast, deep shadows, neon highlights (Cyan/Magenta/Yellow).
Tone: Gritty, Detective Noir meets Brothers Grimm.

3. Unit Roster (Wave 1: Tiers 1-2)

Tribe: Syndicate (Human)
Identity: Organized, Scaling, Economy. "Safety in Numbers".
T1: Beat Cop (2/3) (Reskin of Town Guard)
Effect: None. (Vanilla tank).
T1: Snitch (2/1)
Effect: Deathrattle: Reduce upgrade cost by 1.
T2: Field Medic (2/2) (Reskin of Cleric)
Effect: Battlecry: Give other friendly units +1/+1.
T2: Riot Squad (3/3)
Effect: Taunt.
Tribe: Specter (Undead)
Identity: Sticky, Reborn, Swarm. "Death is just a shift change."
T1: Corpse Rat (1/1) (Reskin of Skeleton)
Effect: Deathrattle: Summon a 1/1 Rat.
T1: Haunted Mirror (0/3)
Effect: Taunt. Deathrattle: Deal 1 dmg to attacker.
T2: Poltergeist (3/2)
Effect: Reborn (Resurrects with 1 HP once).
T2: Grave Robber (2/4)
Effect: Gains +1 Attack whenever a friendly unit dies.
Tribe: Feral (Beast)
Identity: Glass Cannons, Attack Buffs. "The city has teeth."
T1: Stray Dog (2/2)
Effect: Battlecry: Summon a 1/1 Pup.
T1: Sewer Croc (2/3)
Effect: None.
T2: Black Shuck (3/3) (Reskin of Dire Wolf)
Effect: Passive: Adjacent minions have +1 Attack.
T2: Alpha Wolf (3/3)
Effect: Battlecry: Give friendly Ferals +1/+1.
Tribe: Construct (Machine)
Identity: Divine Shields, Buff Receivers. "Built to last."
T1: Traffic Drone (1/2)
Effect: Divine Shield.
T1: Mannequin (1/4)
Effect: Taunt.
T2: Iron John (4/5) (Reskin of Heavy Knight)
Effect: None. (Big stats).
T2: Spark Plug (2/2)
Effect: Passive: Whenever you play a Construct, gain +1 Attack.
