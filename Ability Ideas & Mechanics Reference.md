Ability Ideas & Mechanics Reference

A comprehensive list of mechanics and ability ideas inspired by Hearthstone Battlegrounds and MTG Arena, adapted for Project Grimm.

Core Keywords

Combat

Taunt: Enemies must attack this unit first.
Divine Shield: The first time this unit takes damage, ignore it.
Reborn: The first time this unit dies, resummon it with 1 Health.
Poisonous / Deathtouch: Destroy any minion damaged by this unit.
Windfury / Double Strike: This unit attacks twice.
Cleave: This unit deals damage to the target and its neighbors.
Frenzy: Trigger an effect the first time this unit takes damage and survives.
Overkill: Trigger an effect if this unit deals more damage than the target's health.

Economy & Shop

Battlecry: Effect triggers when you play the unit from hand.
Deathrattle: Effect triggers when the unit dies.
Start of Combat: Effect triggers right before the first attack.
End of Turn: Effect triggers after shopping, before combat.
Avenge (X): Effect triggers after X friendly minions die.

Ability Concepts (Thematic)

Syndicate (Economy/Stats)

"Bribe": Battlecry: Give a minion in the tavern +2/+2.
"Investment": End of Turn: If you have unspent Gold, gain +1/+1.
"Protection Racket": Start of Combat: Give your right-most minion Divine Shield.
"Informant": Deathrattle: The next refresh costs (0).

Specter (Reborn/Death)

"Haunt": Deathrattle: Give a random friendly minion "Deathrattle: Summon a 1/1 Spirit".
"Possession": Battlecry: Destroy a friendly minion to gain its stats.
"Ethereal Form": Taunt. Divine Shield. Has only 1 Health.
"Vengeful Spirit": Avenge (2): Deal 3 damage to the lowest health enemy.

Feral (Swarm/Tokens)

"Pack Leader": Whenever you summon a Beast, give it +2 Attack.
"Alpha's Call": Start of Combat: Summon a 2/2 Wolf for each empty space.
"Scavenger": Whenever a friendly Beast dies, gain +2/+1.
"Rabies": Poisonous.

Construct (Shields/Mechs)

"Repair Bot": End of Turn: Give a friendly Mech +2 Health.
"Magnetize": Battlecry: Choose a friendly Mech. Combine this unit's stats and keywords into it.
"Shield Generator": Avenge (3): Give a random friendly minion Divine Shield.
"Self-Destruct": Deathrattle: Deal 2 damage to all enemy minions.

Arcane (Spells/Scaling)

"Spellweaver": Whenever you cast a spell (Hero Power), gain +1/+1.
"Polymorph": Start of Combat: Transform the enemy's highest attack minion into a 1/1 Sheep.
"Mana Addict": Battlecry: Gain +1/+1 for each Gold you spent this turn.
"Clone": Battlecry: Choose a friendly non-Golden minion. Add a plain copy of it to your hand.

Eldritch (High Risk)

"Blood Pact": Battlecry: Deal 2 damage to your Hero to gain +2 Gold.
"Devourer": Start of Combat: Eat the minion in the tavern across from this one. Gain its stats.
"Chaos Bolt": Deathrattle: Deal 5 damage to a random unit (friendly or enemy).
"Void Portal": End of Turn: Consume a minion in the tavern to gain +2/+2.
