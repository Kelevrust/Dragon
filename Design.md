Project Grimm: Design Bible

1. Visual Identity

Theme: Neon Noir / Urban Folklore.
Setting: "The Sprawl" - A modern metropolis inhabited by myths hiding in plain sight.
Style: High contrast, deep shadows, neon highlights (Cyan/Magenta/Yellow).
Tone: Gritty, Detective Noir meets Brothers Grimm.

4. Implementation Workflow (Unity)
Generate: Run prompts in Midjourney/DALL-E. Generate 4 variations, pick the best.
Upscale: Use the upscale feature to get a clean image.
Process (Optional): Open in Photoshop/GIMP to remove backgrounds if you want transparency (or use a "Remove Background" AI tool).
Import: Drag into Assets/Art/Sprites/Units/[Tribe].
Configure:
Texture Type: Sprite (2D and UI).
Compression: High Quality.
Assign: Drag the sprite into the Artwork slot of your UnitData ScriptableObjects
